[h1]EvE Online: Horizon Lite[/h1]
This mod is a smaller version of EvE Online: Horizon. It will only feature changes to graphical elements and content. It will be updated with new additions from the main mod as they become available and do not alter gameplay.
We will as well attempt to keep compatibility with other mods high.

[h1]Features[/h1]
•	18 pre-scripted factions from EvE Online
•	4 phenotypes with twelve portraits each
•	34 emblems to customize your flags
